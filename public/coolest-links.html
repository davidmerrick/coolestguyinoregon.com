Get Links from other Relevant Sites

Backlinking is probably the most crucial factor in SEO. A great example of a site that ranks exclusively off of backlinking is the Adobe Reader Download Page.

If you search Google for "click here," that site is the first one that shows up despite the fact that the phrase "click here" is not once displayed on the page.

That being said, consider these:

  • Always include your keywords in your link anchor text
  • Bookmark your site on places like Delicious.com, Digg, etc.
  • Put an article on ezinearticles.com or Squidoo.com with a link back to your page
  • Press releases are a great way to get links. You can do these for $200 at places like PRWeb.com
